# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Always verify website URLs before entering personal information
- Never share OTPs or passwords with anyone
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Always check the sender’s email address carefully
- Keep your devices and apps updated regularly

## Financial/UPI Safety
- Do not share UPI PIN or OTP with anyone
- Verify payment requests before approving transactions

## Reporting
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
