# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## Do’s
- Keep your profiles professional
- Share useful and positive content
- Protect your privacy settings
- Verify information before sharing
- Be respectful in online interactions

## Don’ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
- Do not engage in cyberbullying
- Avoid spreading misinformation
- Do not use unprofessional language
